BATMAN, The Dark Knight

About Batman, The Dark Knight

Batman is the secret identity of Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American man, who witnessed his parent's murder as a child. He created this persona to avenge their murder, and bring a sense of justice in the corrupted city of Gotham.

Batman's characteristics

  • Batman doesn't have any superpower, but super cool gadgets,
  • He don't kill people, but bring them to Justice,
  • His costume is bad-ass,
  • He have the coolest side kick ( not Robin ), Alfred,
  • And the coolest enemy, The Joker

Batman ready to kick some asses.

Batman's sidekick

  • Alfred Pennyworth, his butler. He also took care of young Bruce after his parent's death
  • Commissioner James Gordon from the GCPD, shares Batman's goal to fight crime in Gotham
  • Lucius Fox, a technology specialist who supply weapon and gadget to Batman.
  • Robin, his sidekick.
